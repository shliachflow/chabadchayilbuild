Chabad Chayil Building Campaign

Hand in Hand. Foot by Foot!

The new 5 story building will host our current award winning programs: CHAP, CKids, CTeen, GROW, Hebrew School & The Dovid Bryn Library. It will G-d willing include 16 Classrooms, 2 Specialty Classrooms, Adult & Children's Libraries, Indoor Jungle Gym Playground, Social Hall & Rooftop Park! With 37,747 sf of indoor space and 10,000 sf of rooftop park and terraces, it will be a gem for the community.
